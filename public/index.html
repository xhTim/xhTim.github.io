Biography

Xiao Huang (黄逍) is an undergraduate in the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), majoring in physics, and a member of Yan Jici Sci-Tech Elite Class of Physics.

Xiao’s research field of interest is high energy physics. During his undergraduate period, he focuses on research in high energy nuclear physics, especially on the study of heavy ion collisions.
